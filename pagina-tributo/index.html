Life of a Legend

  • 1967 - Born in Aberdeen, Washington.
  • 1983 - Kurt was in high school when he met Krist Novoselic, who became his best friend and later the bassist of Nirvana.
  • 1986 - Kurt recorded demo 'Fecal Matter' with Krist on bass.
  • 1988 - Nirvana formed and recorded their first album Bleach.
  • 1990 - Drummer Dave Grohl joined the band. During this period Nirvana signed to Geffen Records.
  • 1991 - Their second album Nevermind released and was a huge success worldwide. Nevermind's single 'Smells like Teen Spirit' reached the number one spot on Billboard top 100. This was the time when the Nirvana's music came to the main stream and Cobain hailed as "the spokesman of a generation".
  • 1992 - Kurt married to musician Courtney Love. Same year their daughter Frances Bean Cobain was born.
  • 1993 - Nirvana released their third album In Utero.
  • 1994 - Kurt died after writing these words for his fans and family.

Wanting to be someone else is a waste of the person you are. - Kurt Cobain
